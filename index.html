Premier League Transfer Spending Visualisation

What a premier league transfer window this was! The transfer records were sent tumbling by clubs up and down the country, buoyed by a record premier league tv deal. Almost half the clubs broke their own transfer record signings, with Manchester United breaking the world transfer record, set previously by Christiano Ronaldo. Manchester City made their intention of dominance clear with total spend going over 150 million. When Arsenal spend close to a 100million£ in the transfer window, you know the competition is intense. 

Hover over the bubbles to compare transfer fee data for each club along with the players who signed for the amount. Click on the bubbles to highlight a team and outside to close animation. We recommend viewing this on desktop for better experience.
